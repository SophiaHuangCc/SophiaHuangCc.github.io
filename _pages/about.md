Hi, I’m **Sophia Huang**, an M.S. student in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University. My interests are in **robotics, perception, embodied AI, and embedded systems**. Previously, I obtained a B.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering and Robotics from Carnegie Mellon University. I was adviced by Professor Sebastian Scherer at Airlab.

I am currently working on robotics and embodied AI research, with past experience in LiDAR perception, SLAM, and embedded systems. Previously, I worked on real-time 3D LiDAR perception at Google and 4D LiDAR / point-cloud perception at SiLC Technologies.

Research interests
======
* Robotics and embodied AI
* Perception and 3D scene understanding
* LiDAR, SLAM, and sensor systems
* Embedded systems and robot integration
